Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the vital signs?
Our IoT devices are calibrated to medical-grade standards with ±2% accuracy for heart rate and ±1% for SpO₂. Data is validated by our AI model.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with HIPAA and GDPR standards for medical data protection.
How often is data updated?
Real-time vitals update every 5 seconds. Historical data is stored and can be accessed anytime.
Can I export my medical data?
Absolutely! You can export your medical history as CSV from the History page or Settings page.
Can I share my data with my doctor?
Yes! You can generate a shareable report PDF or CSV and send it directly to your healthcare provider.
